One year ago, me, Viola, Francesco found out that it's been a while since we're around in the underground electronic scene.
Also we wanted to contribute with a different type of content that pushed our desire a bit away from music release.

After few months we were playing at "CSA Trenino" in Civitanova Marche, where Marco Philopat was presenting his new book "I Pirati dei navigli".
Ick_ was so inspired by Marco's words. He and Marco had a talk the day after the show. In that exact moment we decided to build something that wouldn't remain in the story archive of **the pics social network**.

### The trip, the connection.



### why a zine?

Social media always has been a good allie in promoting our shows and events, but our anarcopunk background barely kills us each time we post a link, a photo or a story **the pics social network**  because more than ever during these times **politics is important**.

### once upon a time.. [`hypertext`](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/rfc1866/)

Currently (as of early 2026), the three major cloud computing providers (AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform) dominate the global cloud infrastructure market, collectively hosting a share of between 62% and over 70% of the world's web services, including websites, databases, compute clusters, and AI infrastructure. These numbers are oddly terrifying.

We MUST know and remember how Internet was born as a place of freedom of knowledge and expression.

The concept of hyperlink has been replaced with a very virtual mask of it, I'll say _**hypolink**_, and data format is reduced to a dicothomic cage of Post/Story.
