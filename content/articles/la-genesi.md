## It's April 2019

Francesco (Ick_) and I (b00leant) have known each other for years, frequenting the same venues in the capital.
Hardcore punk was our common denominator, but as we chatted, we discovered we had something
in common: chiptune.
Ick_ was already an expert on [LSDj](https://www.littlesounddj.com/lsd/index.php), while I was tinkering with MilkyTracker and using it to soundtrack my Unity video games.
Thanks to the "Micromusic Italy" community, we discovered another chiptuner in Rome, Daimonchip. The guy, a complete stranger at the time, started messaging us and arranged our first meeting in the De Lollis Underground rehearsal room; the first of what would become a long series of meetings where we exchanged valuable knowledge.

We decided to form this collective to play around, without imagining what it would be capable of.
